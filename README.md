# 8917B

**Learn with Armageddon**

Hello! We are team 8917B. We created this website as a guide for all VEX users to deepen their understanding of all things robotics: from notebooking, to building, and even to programming. 

As a team with extensive experience in robot design and construction, programming, and engineering documentation, we've developed effective methods for solving the challenges that every team encounters throughout the season. 

When we first started, many aspects of robotics (from designing a reliable robot to writing an engineering notebook and developing autonomous programs) felt overwhelming. Through years of learning and improving, we've built a systematic problem-solving process that has helped us succeed. Through this, we have qualified for the World Championships numerous times as well as acheiving multiple awards at that level. We created this website to share those strategies with beginner and developing teams.

In this guide, we explain our understanding of the various components that make up the field as we know of it today. However, we mainly emphasize the importance of a teams' design process. Specifically, we break down our own terms and provide explanations to explain the reasoning behind our decisions. We simplify the thought-process behind our programming techniques, notebooking strategies, and troubleshooting methods so that other teams can understand how to approach problems step by step. Our goal is to make robotics more approachable and help teams gain the confidence to tackle challenges on their own.

No matter what step of the robotics journey you're on, we hope this website serves as a practical resource to support your growth and inspire your own innovative solutions.
